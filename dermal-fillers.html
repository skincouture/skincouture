Dermal Fillers

  1. Home
  2. Dermal Fillers

Dermal fillers are a non-surgical cosmetic treatment designed to restore volume, smooth out wrinkles, and enhance facial contours. They are made up of a gel-like substance, usually containing hyaluronic acid, a natural substance found in the body that helps keep the skin hydrated and plump.

Dermal fillers are injected into the skin using a fine needle or cannula. They can be used to treat a variety of areas, including the cheeks, lips, nasolabial folds, marionette lines, and under-eye hollows. They can also be used to add volume to the back of the hands. The procedure is relatively quick and simple, usually taking no more than 30 minutes. Results are immediate, and patients can usually resume their normal activities immediately after the treatment. The effects of dermal fillers typically last from 6-18 months, depending on the type of filler used and the area treated.
