Services

We provide a variety of different services to meet all your medical aesthetic needs

Dermal Fillers

Dermal fillers are injectable substances that can help reduce the appearance of fine lines, wrinkles, and facial folds.

Neurotoxins

Neurotoxins are a type of injectable treatment that temporarily paralyze muscles to reduce the appearance of wrinkles and fine lines on the face.

Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP)

PRP is a cosmetic treatment that uses the patient's own blood to promote skin rejuvenation and reduce signs of aging.

Non-Surgical Thread Lift

A non-surgical thread lift is a minimally invasive cosmetic procedure that uses dissolvable sutures to lift and tighten sagging skin.

Micro Needling

Micro-needling is a cosmetic treatment that uses small needles to puncture the skin and stimulate collagen production for a smoother appearance.
